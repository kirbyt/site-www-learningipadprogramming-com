Upcoming Boston Area Meetups and Events

iOS and Mac

NSHappyHour
NSHappyHour is a monthly happy hour for Mac and iOS developers and for those interested in all things Apple. Meets on the first Wednesday of each month in Salem, MA.

CocoaHeads Boston
CocoaHeads is a monthly gathering of Cocoa developers for Mac OS X and iOS. The group meets on the second Thursday of each month at MIT, Cambridge, MA.

Drinks On Tap
DrinksOnTap is an informal event for Boston area iOS developers. Meets every two to three months, usually in Cambridge, MA. Check the DrinksOnTap.org website for upcoming events.

MacTechGroup
The MacTechGroup has its roots in the old BCS and we just kept going. It’s designed for Macintosh and iOS programmers, iPhone and iPad technical discussions. The group meets on the first Wednesday of each month in Cambridge, MA.

Others

Boston Indies
Boston Indies is a monthly meet up for independent and aspiring game developers.

Refresh Boston
Refresh is a community of designers and developers working to refresh the creative, technical, & professional culture of New Media endeavors in the Boston area.
